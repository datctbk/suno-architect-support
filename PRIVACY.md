# Privacy Policy for Suno Architect

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Overview

Suno Architect ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The App operates entirely on your device and does not:
- Create user accounts
- Store personal data on servers
- Track your location
- Access your contacts or photos
- Use analytics or tracking tools

## How the App Works

When you use Suno Architect:

1. **Song Prompts** — Your song ideas and prompts are processed through Google's Gemini AI API to generate results. These requests are made directly from your device and are not stored by us.

2. **Advertisements** — The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Google Gemini AI** — For prompt generation ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Google AdMob** — For advertisements ([AdMob Privacy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543))

## Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to secure on our end. All API communications use secure HTTPS connections.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
